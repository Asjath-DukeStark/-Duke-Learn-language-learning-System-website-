A Grammer of the Arabic language

This is a seminal work in the field of Arabic linguistics. Published in the 19th century, this comprehensive grammar book offers a detailed analysis of Arabic grammar, syntax, and morphology. Wright's grammar provides a thorough examination of the structure and rules of classical Arabic, as well as its dialects. Through clear explanations and numerous examples, the book serves as an invaluable resource for students, scholars, and anyone interested in the Arabic language. Wright's meticulous scholarship and linguistic expertise have established his grammar as a classic reference in the study of Arabic linguistics.

William Wright
